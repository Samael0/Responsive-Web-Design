Introduction

Start off at the Rope Bridge Cave bonfire in The Painted World of Ariandel area. From the bonfire head across the rope bridge. The enemies here will not attack you, but you can slaughter them if you wish.

Just ahead there’s an NPC (who you will fight later on). Before you talk to the NPC, circle around to the right to find a corpse holding a Hollow Gem.

  • Speak to the NPC, then head through the double doors behind him. Light the Ariandel Chapel bonfire and speak with Lady Friede in the corner.
  • You can head up the nearby ladder, but there’s nothing in the room above just yet. There will be an NPC there later so remember this location.

For now, head back to the rope bridge and cut it down. This causes the bridge to turn into a ladder that you can climb down.

Area 2

Kill the lizard to obtain a Twinkling Titanite, but be very careful here. In addition to the knights on the ledge near the lizard, there are two more large knights with greatbows nearby.

One is very near the ground level, and the other is perched on a branch above you. Stay on the move to avoid the various projectile attacks while you move around this area.

Defeat the first lesser knight, the head across the next branch to find an item corpse on the far side that holds an Ember. Turn around and head to the immediate right, hugging the cliff side as you inch along the narrow path.
